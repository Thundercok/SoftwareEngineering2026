## 📌 Project Overview
Hệ thống **Máy bán vé tự động (Ticket Vendor Machine - TVM)** dành cho tuyến Metro Bến Thành (TP.HCM). Hệ thống hỗ trợ mua vé xe buýt và tàu điện ngầm (MRT) với các phương thức thanh toán hiện đại: Thẻ tín dụng (Credit Card) và mã QR (MoMo, VNPay, ZaloPay).

**Database:** Microsoft SQL Server (MSSQL)
**Design Tools:** Draw.io, Figma, Canva

Yes you can — VietQR is actually just a QR code standard, not a payment gateway. It doesn't need a developer account.
Here's how it works:

VietQR is just a formatted QR code containing your bank account info + amount. The customer scans it with their banking app, and their app pre-fills the transfer. You don't get an automatic webhook — the bank just shows the transfer in your account.
So the real question is: how do you confirm payment was received?

Your classmate probably did one of two things:
